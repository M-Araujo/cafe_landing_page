Veggie Stew

A hearty and nutritious dish packed with a variety of vegetables simmered in a flavorful broth. Perfect for a cozy meal, this stew is both satisfying and wholesome, offering a burst of flavors with each bite.

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Potatoes
  • Zucchini
  • Bell pepper
  • Green beans
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Vegetable broth
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary
  • Paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chickpeas
  • Peas
  • Fresh parsley

Roasted Potatoes

A classic and comforting side dish, known for its crispy exterior and tender interior. Seasoned with a blend of herbs and spices, then baked to golden perfection, making them an ideal accompaniment to any meal.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Fresh rosemary
  • Fresh thyme

Apple Pie

A timeless dessert that features a buttery, flaky crust filled with tender, spiced apples. This classic treat is often enjoyed warm, sometimes with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream, making it a perfect end to any meal.

Ingredients:

  • Apples (Granny Smith, Honeycrisp, or a mix)
  • Granulated sugar
  • Packed brown sugar
  • All-purpose flour
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Ground nutmeg
  • Ground allspice (optional)
  • Lemon juice
  • Unsalted butter
  • Eggs
  • Pie crusts

Risotto

A creamy Italian rice dish cooked to perfection with broth, wine, and Parmesan cheese. Known for its rich texture and delightful flavor, risotto can be customized with various ingredients such as mushrooms, seafood, or vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • Arborio rice
  • Vegetable broth
  • Dry white wine
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Unsalted butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley
  • Mushrooms, asparagus, and peas
